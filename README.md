#Phishing-Email

## Objective


The Phishing Lab project aimed to establish a controlled environment for simulating and detecting cyber Phishing Email attempt. The primary focus was to view a real-time Phishing Email and deal with it and gain awareness. This hands-on experience was designed to deepen understanding of Phishing attack patterns, and defensive strategies.

### Skills Learned

- Proficiency in analyzing suspicious Emails.
- Ability to recognize Phishing signatures and patterns.
- Development of critical thinking and problem-solving skills in cybersecurity.

### Tools Used

- Email analysis tools (such as MXToolBox) for capturing and examining Emails.
- tools to check IP location and holder (such as IPabuser).
